Black Thunder

Black Thunder is not just another water theme park, it is the grandest, greatest of them all. With a backdrop of the Nilgiris, Black Thunder Water Theme Park in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is a place of unlimited fun and adventure. On offer are a combination of land games and water games in the largest wave pool in India. Be ready to experience exotic camel rides, poolside frolic, video games and a ride in the 1.8 Kilometre long moat that surrounds the park.
